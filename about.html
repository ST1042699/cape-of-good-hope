Our History

Cape of Good Hope SPCA was founded 20 May 1872 as a society to prevent cruelty towards Animals. Cape Town is our main founding society of this SPCA movement in South Africa as well as one of the oldest welfare facility

Mission Statement

Our mission is to prevent cruelty that happens towards animals as well as promote the welfare of all animals that have been absued, neglected and abdoned by doing this showing compassion by providing veterinary care as well as edcuation about animals abuse we want to make sure all animals are safe and well taken care of because no animals desereves to be without a home

our vision

Our vision is to make sure that there is an end to Animal creulty in South Africa our main goal is to ensure that animals that have the love and care they deserve by raising awareness about animal abuse and neglect. As well as the animals that have been abandoned we strive to ensure all our animals are happy and healty and ensure that they find happy and loving homes.
